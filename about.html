About

Researching the intersection of visualization and human-computer interaction

I lean towards things at the intersection of computation and design. Meaning, I like to work on projects as a software developer and designer with a focus on information visualization.

I am interested how interactive visualizations can help breakdown complex topics and promote storytelling and insight. Broadly speaking, I am interested on projects that promote the interplay of human, machine, and data.

I am currently a Master's student in Computer Science at University of California, Davis. I work with Professor Kwan-Liu Ma in the Visualization and Interface Design Innovation research group (VIDi) and Professor Hao-Chuan Wang in the Collaborative and Social computing Lab (CSC Lab).
